# Components

**NOTE:** I have not decided strict components because I am not on the frontend team and I want to give you guys the flexibility to manage your side of the project your style. Though I would advise for you to at least componentize the Product card(s) and also make two versions of it one for list view (shopping cart, wishlist) and one for card view (main page, products page).
